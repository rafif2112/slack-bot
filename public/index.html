Available Slash Commands

Type these commands in any channel to interact with Fidev Bot.

/fidev-ping

Check bot latency and connectivity.

/fidev-catfact

Get a random, interesting cat fact.

/fidev-joke

Brighten your day with a random joke.

/fidev-hello

A friendly greeting from your bot.

/fidev-echo

Bot will repeat whatever you say.

/fidev-quote

Get an inspirational quote to stay motivated.

/fidev-help

Show all available commands directly in Slack.
